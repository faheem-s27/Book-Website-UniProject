13 hours

Your home was the one place you felt safe. Now it’s your prison. You were invisible. Now your every move is monitored by masked strangers. You looked after your mum, your best friend. Now you barely know her. Everything can change in thirteen hours.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix is a book about a boy wizard who returns to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for his fifth year, where he must confront the return of the evil Lord Voldemort and a new teacher who seems determined to undermine him.

Diary of a Wimpy Kid Old School

"Diary of a Wimpy Kid: Old School" is a book about a middle schooler named Greg Heffley who is forced to go "off the grid" for a week as part of a school assignment. During this time, he learns how to live without modern technology and discovers some surprising things about his family's history.

Wreck this Journal

"Wreck This Journal" is an interactive book that encourages its readers to engage in creative destruction. Each page contains a different prompt or instruction, such as "rip this page out" or "spill a drink here," and encourages the reader to experiment and think outside the box.
